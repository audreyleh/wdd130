Down the River

Are you ready to embark on an adrenaline-pumping journey through the rapids? Look no further than our thrilling rafting adventures! At Down the River, we offer a variety of exciting trips that cater to all levels of experience. Whether you're a novice seeking a leisurely float down the river or a seasoned rafter craving the challenge of Class V rapids, we have the perfect excursion for you.

Our Rafting Adventures

Beginner's Delight:
Duration: 2 hours
Description: Perfect for first-timers and families, this gentle rafting trip takes you through scenic stretches of the river, where you can admire the beauty of nature while floating effortlessly downstream. Our expert guides will provide basic instructions and ensure your safety at all times.


Intermediate Expedition:
Duration: Half-day (4 hours)
Description: Ready to step up your rafting game? Join us on an intermediate-level adventure featuring moderate rapids and thrilling twists and turns. This trip offers the perfect balance of excitement and relaxation, with plenty of opportunities to test your skills and soak in the stunning surroundings.


Advanced Challenge:
Duration: Full-day (6-8 hours)
Description: Calling all adrenaline junkies! Brace yourself for an adrenaline-fueled journey through the most intense rapids on the river. Our advanced rafting trip is designed for experienced paddlers seeking the ultimate thrill. Conquer exhilarating Class IV and V rapids as you navigate through rugged terrain and heart-pounding drops.


Equipment Available:

  • Rafts: Our fleet of high-quality inflatable rafts is designed to withstand the rigors of whitewater rafting, ensuring a safe and comfortable ride for all adventurers.
  • Paddles: Each participant will be equipped with a sturdy paddle, essential for navigating the rapids and steering your raft with precision.
  • Personal Flotation Devices (PFDs): Safety is our top priority. We provide PFDs for all participants, ensuring that everyone stays afloat in the event of a capsize.
  • Helmets: Protect your noggin with our durable helmets, designed to safeguard against potential hazards on the river.
  • Wet Suits and Splash Gear: Stay warm and dry with our selection of wet suits and splash gear, available for rent to keep you comfortable throughout your rafting adventure.
