Down the River

Rafting and whitewater rafting are recreational outdoor activities which use an inflatable raft to navigate a river or other body of water. This is often done on whitewater or different degrees of rough water. Dealing with risk is often a part of the experience.

History

Down the River rafting is known and loved for being the first rafting opportunities each spring in the U.S. thanks to the early snowmelt in the mountains! Down the River rafting adventures offer a truly unique experience; with the gorgeous high desert scenery and wildlife and the chance to really get away from it all on remote wilderness trips.
