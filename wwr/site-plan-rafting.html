Overview

Purpose

Considered an extreme water sport, the sport is synonymous with adrenaline and can be practiced by anyone who wants to brave rapids aboard a boat. The objective of this website is to make it possible for Rafting lovers to find a place, all of this in an accessible and easy way.

Audience

We want to make it possible for children, young people and adults to have the pleasure of experiencing the adrenaline rush of going down the river. Who are the target customers? Children, young people and adults. People of all ages who like to adventure. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? What types of Rafting do we offer, what days and times will we be serving the public, and how can they prepare to have a memorable day. How will my audience usually access my site ? The website will be accessed through any device, be it mobile, desktop or laptop.
